FAQ

Do I need to pay any money to register for iOS Fusion?

Yes. Since this is a Flagship Event of our Chapter, we are charging some fees for the iOS Fusion event.

Do I need to have any specific qualifications to be a participant for iOSFusion?

If possible please bring a Mac as it is good for iOS development. Otherwise a Windows laptop will work too! No programming knowledge needed, we will teach you everything.

What should I do after completing registration ?

After registering, you will get an email that will confirm your registration. Keep following our updates on social media and just relax!

What if I experience some technical difficulties while setup, building app etc?

Our trained volunteers will help you if you face any such problems. They will always be there to help you.

What’s the Venue of this Event?

Location

If I register for the event and for some reason I cannot attend the event, can I get a refund?

Refunds are not possible, but please do try to attend the event you’ll learn a lot from it.

How many hours in a day is the event?

Approximately 6 hours.

I have another question that is not answered in your FAQs, what should I do?

We have made a Discord server for this event for which the link will be given to you in your email. Please join that server and ask us your queries on that server. We will be happy to help!
